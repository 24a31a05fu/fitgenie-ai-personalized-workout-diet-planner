# FitGenie AI – Personalized Workout & Diet Planner

## Overview

FitGenie AI is an intelligent fitness and nutrition platform designed to help students achieve their health goals through personalized recommendations.

The system uses AI-powered analysis to generate workout routines, meal plans, calorie recommendations, health insights, and downloadable fitness reports.

---

## Features

### Personalized Workout Generator
- Beginner
- Intermediate
- Advanced
- Home Workout
- Gym Workout

### AI Diet Planner
- Breakfast Plans
- Lunch Plans

### BMI & Health Analysis
- BMI Calculation

- Protein Requirement

### Progress Tracking
